Each guide is built around a job rather than a command: working with videos,
pulling comments and bullet-chat, browsing creators and their feeds, reading
bangumi and audio and columns, watching live rooms, finding things across the
site, discovering the graph of related videos and creators, and crawling it into
a dataset. They assume you have run the
[quick start](/getting-started/quick-start/).
